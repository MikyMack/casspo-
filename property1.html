About Accommodation

Set in 40 acres of serene forest, Casspo Forest Bungalow offers a peaceful retreat just 9 km inside Munnar towards Lakshmi Estate. Accommodating 16 to 30 people, including tent stays, the bungalow is perfect for group getaways. Extra beds and tents are available for larger groups. Enjoy the tranquil weather and natural beauty, making it an ideal destination for relaxation and togetherness.

  • Booking Options :: Available for the entire property or for separate groups.
  • Location :: Casspo Forest Bungalow, Munnar – located 9 km inside Munnar towards Lakshmi Estate.
  • Property Details :: A private forest bungalow spread across 40 acres.
  • Capacity :: 16 to 30 people, including tent stays
  • Additional Accommodation :: Groups exceeding 16 people can be accommodated with extra beds and tent stays.
