About Our Chayathottam

Nestled amidst lush tea plantations, Chaythottam offers a serene retreat where tranquility meets charm. Set in the calm and picturesque landscapes of Munnar, this bungalow invites you to relax and immerse yourself in nature's beauty. Whether you're sipping a warm cup of tea while overlooking the verdant hills or enjoying the peaceful ambiance, Chaythottam promises a rejuvenating escape. Experience the perfect blend of comfort, calm, and the soothing allure of tea gardens, creating a haven to refresh your mind, body, and soul.

Set amidst lush tea plantations, Chaythottam offers a serene escape in the heart of Munnar. With breathtaking views and a peaceful ambiance, it’s the perfect retreat to unwind and recharge. Embrace the beauty of nature and create unforgettable memories in this tranquil haven.
